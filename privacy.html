Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Our commitment: Self Block is designed with privacy at its core. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the buddy and community features. Your screen time data and app usage information never leave your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data stored on your device only (never sent to our servers):

  • App usage and screen time statistics (managed by Apple's Screen Time API)
  • Blocked app selections and blocking schedules
  • Daily check-in entries (mood, notes, goals)
  • Shield theme preferences

1.2 Data stored on our servers (Firebase):

  • Anonymous account ID: A random identifier created automatically — no email, name, or personal information required
  • Nickname: A display name you choose (can be anything)
  • Invite code: A unique code for connecting with buddies
  • Buddy connections: Which anonymous IDs are paired as buddies
  • Access requests: When you or your buddy request temporary access to a blocked app (includes optional short messages)
  • Push notification token: For delivering buddy request notifications
  • Timezone offset: To send notifications at appropriate local times

1.3 Optional data (only if you choose):

  • Apple Sign-In: If you link your Apple ID for account recovery. We only receive a relay email address and a unique identifier — Apple does not share your real email with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable the buddy system — connecting you with a friend for mutual accountability
  • To enable the community mode — anonymous access requests to the community
  • To deliver push notifications about buddy requests and responses
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To send helpful reminders at appropriate times (using timezone offset)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your real name, email, phone number, or any personal contact information
  • We do NOT collect which specific apps you block or use
  • We do NOT collect your screen time data or usage statistics
  • We do NOT collect your location
  • We do NOT use any advertising SDKs or trackers
  • We do NOT sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for advertising or marketing

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database (Firestore), push notifications (FCM), analytics, crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Firebase Analytics collects anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g., app opens, feature usage) to help us improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Data Retention

  • Access requests are automatically deleted after they expire (typically within minutes to hours)
  • Your account data is retained as long as you use the app
  • If you uninstall the app without linking Apple Sign-In, your anonymous account becomes inaccessible
  • You can request deletion of all your data by contacting us

6. Data Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard measures:

  • All server communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firebase security rules ensure users can only access their own data and their buddy's shared data
  • Sensitive data (Apple Sign-In credentials) is stored in the iOS Keychain

7. Children's Privacy

Self Block is intended for users aged 4 and above (with parental guidance for minors). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The anonymous authentication system means no personal data is required to use the app.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics (by disabling analytics in your device settings)
  • Disconnect from buddies at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion:

Email: umaysoft@gmail.com

  • サブスクリプションの状態を管理するため
  • 適切なタイミングで役立つリマインダーを送信するため（タイムゾーンオフセットを使用）

  • お客様の本名、メールアドレス、電話番号、またはその他の個人的な連絡先情報は収集しません
  • お客様がブロックまたは使用している特定のアプリに関する情報は収集しません
  • お客様のスクリーンタイムデータまたは使用統計は収集しません
  • お客様の位置情報は収集しません
  • 広告SDKやトラッカーは一切使用しません
  • 広告またはマーケティング目的で第三者にデータを販売、共有、または転送することはありません

4. サードパーティサービス

以下のサードパーティサービスを使用しています：

  • Firebase（Google）：認証、データベース（Firestore）、プッシュ通知（FCM）、アナリティクス、クラッシュレポート。Firebaseプライバシーポリシー

  • アクセスリクエストは有効期限が切れた後、自動的に削除されます（通常、数分から数時間以内）
  • お客様のアカウントデータはアプリを使用している間保持されます

  • お問い合わせいただくことで、すべてのデータの削除をリクエストできます

6. データセキュリティ

お客様のデータは業界標準の対策で保護されています：

  • すべてのサーバー通信はHTTPS/TLS暗号化を使用しています
  • Firebaseセキュリティルールにより、ユーザーは自分自身のデータとバディの共有データにのみアクセスできます
  • 機密データ（Appleでサインインの認証情報）はiOSキーチェーンに保存されます
